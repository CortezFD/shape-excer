Stress Relief

We all encounter stress at one time or another. So it's improtant to be able to recognize stress and learn how to deal with it

How to manage your stress

To help manage stress, try the following:

  • Take a break from what you're doing
  • Exercise on a regular basis
  • Take time to smile and laugh
  • Get support from family and friends
  • Meditate
